# Dhadhan Cafe ☕

Ku soo dhowow **Dhadhan Cafe**, shabakad casri ah oo loogu talagalay maqaayad qaxwada laga cabo (coffee shop), taasi oo bixisa khibrad isticmaale oo heer sare ah, naqshad indho-qabad leh, iyo sifooyin la falgalaya isticmaalaha (interactive features).

## 📝 Faahfaahin
Mashruucan waa website hal bog ah (One-page website) oo si buuxda u shaqeynaya (responsive), waxaana lagu dhisay **HTML5**, **CSS3**, iyo **JavaScript**. Waxuu leeyahay naqshad nadiif ah, midabyo dabiici ah oo la xiriira qaxwada (Caramel & Coffee Dark), iyo animation-no soo jiidasho leh.

## ✨ Astaamaha Muhiimka ah (Features)
*   **Hero Section**: Waxaa ku jira qoraal soo jiidasho leh iyo badhan "Surprise Me (AI)" oo soo jeedinaya cabitaan aan horey loo sii qorsheyn.
*   **About Us**: Qeyb ka hadleysa qisada Dhadhan Cafe, sawir tayo leh, iyo astaamo (icons) muujinaya tayada adeega.
*   **Menu Interactive ah**: Liiska cabitaanada iyo macmacaanka oo loo kala saaray 'Tabs' (Qaxwo, Cabitaan, Macmacaan), si fududna loo dhex-mari karo.
*   **Signature Combo**: Soo bandhigida dalab gaar ah (Combo) oo leh qiimo dhimis iyo muuqaal soo jiidasho leh.
*   **Stats Counter**: Tirokoobyo (Stats) nool oo iskood isu tirinaya marka shaashadda la soo gaaro.
*   **Mobile Responsive**: Menu-ga mobilka oo si qurux badan u soo dhacaya, iyo badhanka "Dalbo Hadda" oo mobilka u gaar ah.
*   **Floating Action Buttons (FAB)**: Badhamo sabeynaya (WhatsApp & Call) oo macaamiisha u ogolaanaya in ay si toos ah ula xiriiraan maqaayadda.

## 🛠 Tignoolajiyada La Isticmaalay
*   **HTML5**: Dhismaha bogga.
*   **CSS3**: Qurxinta, Animation-ka, iyo Variable-da midabyada.
*   **JavaScript (Vanilla)**: Shaqooyinka sida Menu-ga mobilka, Tab-yada, iyo Counter-ka.
*   **FontAwesome**: Astaamaha (Icons).
*   **Google Fonts**: Farta (Outfit & Inter).

## 🚀 Sida Loo Isticmaalo
Si aad u aragto mashruucan, kaliya fur file-ka `index.html` adiga oo isticmaalaya browser kasta (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, iwm).

1.  Soo degso (Clone) repository-gan ama soo rogo (download) faylasha.
2.  Fur `index.html`.
3.  Ku raaxeyso "Vibe-ka" Dhadhan Cafe!

## 📱 Mobile View
Website-kan wuxuu si gaar ah u tixgelinayaa taleefannada gacanta. Wuxuu leeyahay 'Hamburger Menu' si fiican u shaqeynaya iyo naqshad la jaanqaadeysa shaashadaha yaryar.
